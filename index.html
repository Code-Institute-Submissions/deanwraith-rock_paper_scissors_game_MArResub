Rock,Paper,Scissors,Lizard and Spock

Rock, Paper and Scissors is played between two people, in which each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with an outstretched hand. Rock is a closed fist, Paper is a flat hand and Scissors is shown with a "V" made with the index and middle finger. Each round has 3 outcomes win, loss or draw depending on the select shape from each player.

The adapted version of the game that includes 2 additional shapes which are Lizard and Spock was created by Sam Kass and Karen Bryla in the early 2000's. Lizard is made by forming a mouth with the hand and Spock is shown by making a the Vulcan salute from Star Trek.
